====================================================
FILE  : SpawnerEvents.cs
SYSTEM: Spawner System (issue #25)
NAMESPACE: GameplayMechanicsUMFOSS.World
====================================================

PURPOSE
Lightweight  struct  payload types for  EventBus.Publish  / subscribe. Spawners
never force other systems to reference them — listeners opt in to these events.

GROUPS
- Wave lifecycle:  OnWaveStartedEvent ,  OnWaveClearedEvent ,  OnAllWavesCompleteEvent
- Instance lifecycle:  OnSpawnedObjectDiedEvent
- Timed:  OnTimedSpawnTriggeredEvent ,  OnTimedSpawnCapReachedEvent
- Proximity:  OnProximitySpawnTriggeredEvent ,  OnProximitySpawnerResetEvent
- Shared:  OnSpawnerStartedEvent ,  OnSpawnerStoppedEvent ,  OnSpawnCountChangedEvent

CROSS-REFERENCES
Published from  WaveSpawner_UMFOSS ,  TimedSpawner_UMFOSS ,  ProximitySpawner_UMFOSS .
Subscribers live anywhere that calls  EventBus.Subscribe<...> .
